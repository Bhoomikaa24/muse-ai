SLIDE 5: WHY WE CHOSE RULE-BASED (NOT AI)
Timing: 45 seconds

Slide Content:
THE CASE FOR INTELLIGENT RULES OVER AI APIS

Rule-Based Advantages:
✓ Speed: 5-10ms vs 1-5 seconds (AI)
✓ Cost: Free vs per-call API charges
✓ Reliability: Deterministic results
✓ Quality: Consistent, predictable output
✓ Privacy: Client-side only, zero data collection
✓ Transparency: Rules can be audited

AI API Limitations:
✗ Slow: 1-5 seconds per generation
✗ Expensive: Ongoing API costs
✗ Unpredictable: Different results each time
✗ Privacy issues: Data sent to third parties
✗ Unreliable: Quality varies
✗ Dependent on external services

Our Philosophy:
Smart design + intelligent rules = Professional results
Without cost, privacy issues, or unpredictability of AI

Speaker Notes:
"Many projects now default to AI, but we made a deliberate choice to use intelligent rule-based generation. This decision delivers superior results. Speed: our system generates in 5 to 10 milliseconds, while AI APIs take 1 to 5 seconds. Cost: we have zero operational costs, while AI requires per-call charges. Reliability: our results are deterministic, always the same for the same input, while AI gives different results each time. Quality: our templates are crafted and tested, while AI quality varies. Privacy: everything runs client-side with zero data collection, while AI requires sending data to third parties. Transparency: our rules can be audited and verified. This demonstrates that intelligent design and well-crafted business logic can deliver professional results without the cost, complexity, and privacy concerns of AI. That's a key learning from this project."
